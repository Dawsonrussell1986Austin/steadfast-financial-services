President & Owner

Matthew McGahey

CFP® · ChFC® · RICP® · CKA®

Matt joined Steadfast on January 1, 2013 and became President and Owner on January 1, 2022. Prior to joining the firm he spent five years at Enterprise Rent‑a‑Car, where he distinguished himself in service and branch management. Between 2013 and 2017 he earned the CFP®, ChFC®, and RICP® designations from The American College, and more recently added the CKA® designation focused on financial wisdom.

Matt listens closely to clients, applying thoughtful wisdom and perspective to financial decisions. He is driven by a commitment to serve others and values the trust and lasting relationships that develop over time. Matt is committed to remaining Steadfast, grounded in strong training, experience, and time‑tested principles.

Education

Bachelor of Science in Business Management, University of Central Florida (2008)

Personal

Matt is happily married to Joy and has three sons — Caleb, Zachary, and Keegan. He's active in Grace Church Orlando and involved in its foster family ministry. Outside of work he enjoys fishing, surfing, college football, golf, running, and coaching basketball.
